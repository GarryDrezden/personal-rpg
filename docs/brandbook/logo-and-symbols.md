# Logo and Symbols

## Concept

Логотип основан на **воронах Одина — Huginn и Muninn** (мысль и память).

Direction: dark fantasy heraldic, не corporate flat.

## Visual elements

- Два ворона / силуэт пары воронов
- Runic / heraldic accents
- Palette: gold, graphite, deep purple (dark theme)
- Readable at 50px (menu) and 120px (hero branding)

## Do not

- Copy personal tattoo photos into public repo
- Use real photo textures
- Cartoon crows / emoji style
