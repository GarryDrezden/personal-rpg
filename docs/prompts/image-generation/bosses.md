## Style

Larger than mobs — **chapter milestone** entities.

```
Stylized realistic 3D dark fantasy boss character,
premium mobile RPG, imposing but not horror,
transparent background, full figure, readable at 256px,
unified art direction across all chapter bosses.
```

## Narrative tone

Bosses represent **chapters of the path**, not personal failure.

Examples:

- **Divan King** — throne of comfort, ruler of inertia
- **Old Form Guardian** — guards previous self, must be acknowledged not destroyed
- **Chain of Rollback** — linked habits pulling back

## vs Weekly trials

These assets are for **Journey / Codex**, not the random weekly challenge cards.
